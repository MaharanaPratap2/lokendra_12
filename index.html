Plant Care Guide

Essential tips for happy, healthy plants

Light Requirements

Most houseplants prefer bright, indirect light. South or east-facing windows are ideal. Rotate plants regularly for even growth.

Watering

Water when the top inch of soil is dry. Overwatering is the #1 killer of houseplants. Use room temperature water.

Temperature

Most plants thrive between 65-75°F (18-24°C). Avoid drafts and sudden temperature changes.

Humidity

Tropical plants love humidity. Group plants together, use a pebble tray, or invest in a humidifier.

Fertilizing

Feed during growing season (spring-summer) with diluted fertilizer. Reduce or stop in winter.

Pest Control

Check regularly for pests. Treat with insecticidal soap or neem oil at first sign of infestation.
